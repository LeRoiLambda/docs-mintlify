## API Categories

### [Stations](/api-reference/stations/list-available-charging-stations)
Access real-time information about charging stations, including availability, pricing, and amenities. Perfect for building applications that help drivers locate and use charging points.

### [Sessions](/api-reference/sessions/start-a-charging-session)
Manage charging sessions from start to finish. Control charging operations, retrieve session details, and handle reservations for a seamless charging experience.

### [Payments](/api-reference/payments/create-a-charging-payment-request)
Handle all payment-related operations, from processing charges to managing refunds and payment methods. Secure and flexible payment integration for your charging services.

### [Subscriptions & Fleet Accounts](/api-reference/fleet/create-fleet-account)
Manage fleet accounts and subscriptions for business customers. Track usage, handle billing, and maintain account balances for fleet operations.

### [Developer Tools & Webhooks](/api-reference/developer-tools/create-api-key)
Access essential developer resources and set up real-time notifications for your application. Stay updated with charging events and system changes.

### [Webhook Events](/api-reference/webhooks/list-webhook-events)
Subscribe to real-time events for charging sessions, payments, and system status. Keep your application synchronized with the charging network.

### [Vehicle & Driver Integration](/api-reference/vehicles/list-connected-vehicles)
Connect with vehicles and manage driver profiles. Access vehicle data, charging history, and battery status for a connected charging experience.
